Carpenter

Ruslan Lypovskyi

Many years ago, when it became necessary to radically change my life, including my profession, I started doing what I always liked, what I was passionate about, namely carpentry.
This was not the beginning, only a continuation, a continuation of the family business. If my grandfather worked only with wood, and my uncle with metal, I combined all the skills acquired since childhood to translate into reality all creative ideas and non-standard solutions for the manufacture of custom furniture.
The greatest pleasure for me was seeing the satisfied faces of my customers when they received a product that usually exceeded their expectations. And this is not surprising, because when you do what you like, you live this project, you put your soul and strength into it.
Although I was never really 100% happy with the result, I always saw something I could have done better. But I think that this is not a shortcoming, but rather an experience. Each project is individual and unique. Each project is a part of my life, my personal story.
